Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

Introduction

At Clean My Prompt, we are committed to transparency and your privacy. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website. Unlike most web applications, we use minimal cookies and no tracking scripts.

All data processing happens locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your sensitive data to any servers.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences and improve user experience. Cookies can be:

  • First-party cookies: Set by the website you're visiting
  • Third-party cookies: Set by external services (we do NOT use these)
  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain until expiry or manual deletion

Cookies We Use

We use only essential cookies to provide core functionality. Here's a complete breakdown:

Cookie Name Purpose Type Duration
theme Remembers your theme preference (dark or warm mode) First-party, Essential Persistent (1 year)
cookieConsent Records your cookie consent choice First-party, Essential Persistent (1 year)
customPatterns Stores your custom regex patterns (session only) Session Storage Session (deleted on close)

⚠️ Important: We do NOT use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking scripts. Your sanitized text never leaves your browser.

Anonymous Analytics

We collect minimal, anonymous analytics to understand how people use our tool. This helps us improve the service. We only track:

  • Country-level location: Which country visitors are from (e.g., "United States", "Germany")
  • Page views: Which pages are visited most often
  • Referral source: How users found us (e.g., Google, direct, social media)

We do NOT track:

  • Individual IP addresses (anonymized immediately)
  • User behavior or clicks
  • Personal information
  • Your sanitized text or prompts
  • Browser fingerprints

Third-Party Services

We use the following external services (loaded from CDNs):

  • Tailwind CSS: For styling (no cookies, no tracking)
  • Google Fonts (Inter): For typography (no cookies, no tracking)
  • Compromise.js (unpkg.com): For NLP processing (no cookies, no tracking)

These services are loaded from public CDNs and do not track your activity on our site.

Your Cookie Choices

You have full control over cookies:

1. Browser Settings

You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or alert you when cookies are being set. However, some features may not work properly without cookies:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

2. Clear Cookies

You can delete all cookies at any time through your browser settings. This will reset:

  • Theme preference (revert to default dark mode)
  • Cookie consent choice (banner will reappear)
  • Custom regex patterns (will be lost)

GDPR & Privacy Regulations

We are committed to compliance with privacy regulations including GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and other international data protection laws.

Your Rights:

  • Right to Access: All data is stored locally on your device. You can inspect it in browser DevTools.
  • Right to Deletion: Clear your browser cookies/storage to delete all data.
  • Right to Portability: All data is in standard browser storage formats.
  • Right to Object: You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
